SPRING SUMMER 2016

Dapo Desina Atelier presents ‘’SIREN’’ the all-time glamorous and festive collection. The look book features elegant and subtly seductive dresses, alongside jumpsuits exhibiting aesthetics of Gatsby and the 1920s. Dapo Lawal, the self-taught designer, who is a graduate of geology and mining, describes the collection as stylishly vintage with the use of sequinned fabrics to create structured dresses, revealing cut outs, extra-long sleeves and faux leather tassel fringes. Every woman will surely find a piece of herself in this collection. Enjoy!
